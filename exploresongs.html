Upload Requirements

To upload your beats to ProducerStack, you need to provide the following:

  • Producer Name: Your stage name or real name
  • Beat Name: Title of your beat
  • Beat File: MP3 format (high quality)
  • Album Artwork: Square image (min 1000x1000px)
  • Genre: Primary genre of the beat
  • Price: Your selling price in Rands
  • Contact Details: WhatsApp number for buyers

All beats must be your original work. You retain 100% ownership and rights to your music.

Upload Fee: R15 per Beat

Each beat listing costs R15 and remains active for 12 months. This fee covers hosting and listing on our platform.
